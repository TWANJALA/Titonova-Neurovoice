## One-Page Sales Pitch for Schools and Clinics

### Title
Titonova NeuroVoice for Schools and Clinical Teams
AAC support that improves daily communication and gives your team measurable outcomes.

### Problem
Schools, therapy programs, and pediatric care teams often face the same challenge: communication support is fragmented across home, classroom, and clinic. Children may use one workflow in therapy, another at school, and a different one at home. That inconsistency makes progress harder to sustain and harder to measure.

### Solution
Titonova NeuroVoice closes that gap.

It is an AI-powered AAC platform designed for non-verbal and semi-verbal children, with tools for caregivers, therapists, and administrators in one connected system. Children get a fast, low-friction communication workspace. Professionals get structured dashboards, goal-tracking workflows, and exportable reporting to support intervention decisions.

### What Your Team Gets
- Child-focused AAC workspace: Sentence builder, word boards, quick phrases, and one-tap speak actions for daily use.
- Adaptive communication intelligence: Smart suggestions and phrase ranking adapt to each child’s usage patterns over time.
- Therapist workspace: Caseload visibility, vocabulary set assignments, goal updates, and child-level outcome exports.
- Admin and population dashboards: Cohort views, risk trend visibility, therapist summaries, and exportable leadership reports.
- Cross-setting continuity: One platform for home, school, and therapy teams.

### Why Schools and Clinics Choose NeuroVoice
1. Supports both day-to-day communication and structured outcome tracking.
2. Usable for families while still giving professionals the depth they need.
3. Scales from individual child plans to organization-wide reporting.

### Common Use Cases
- Special education communication support plans.
- Pediatric speech and language therapy programs.
- Multi-site therapy organizations tracking intervention outcomes.
- School-clinic-family collaboration for AAC consistency.

### Implementation Approach
Start with a focused pilot cohort, define baseline communication goals, and monitor usage and outcome trends over a fixed reporting window. Expand by classroom, caseload, or site as your team validates results.

### Call to Action
If your school or clinic wants stronger AAC consistency and clearer communication outcomes, Titonova NeuroVoice supports both child experience and program-level decision-making in one platform.
